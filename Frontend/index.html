ABOUT

Created by Harmeet Singh, 'Good' is a branded clothing project made to express a simple but powerful form of minimalistic fashion. Leaning towards unbranded clothing, 'Good' offers favor to simplistic fashion choices, matching sets and comfortable streetwear clothing with a positive outlook and nice touch.
The first 'Good' drop was in August 2024, consisting of the original branded yellow passionfruit logo on black champion crafted crew necks and hoodies. The logo embodies a nice soul behind the wearer.
